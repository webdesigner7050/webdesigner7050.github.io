About Us

This is what we do.

Caring hands daycare provides services which contribute to the overall welfare of your child. Our services include: ⁃ Individual attention to each child’s needs ⁃ Spaces where children can create, invent and learn ⁃ A quiet space and art station ⁃ Progressive developmental activities of a Montessori environment

OUR ENVIRONMENT

We, at Caring Hands Daycare, strive to provide a clean, safe and educational environment for young children and their families. Within our care, children develop skills that promote physical, emotional, social and intellectual growth. We believe in being supportive of our parents, and we welcome their positive involvement and presence at all times. We further believe that within our environment, children can thrive and have the opportunity to blossom into responsible and contributing members of our society.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Caring Hands Daycare is dedicated and committed to our children by providing exceptional education and quality child care. It is our philosophy to focus on the needs of the individual child by providing a stimulating atmosphere for learning along with a secure loving environment. It is our belief that a childcare facility should be an extension of each child’s family. Caring Hands Daycare’s teachers and parents are encouraged to work together to provide the love and support needed as your child reaches developmental milestones and his/her own personal achievements. We are passionate about education and providing our children with the tools to be successful in life.
